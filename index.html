Privacy Policy for Slumber App

Slumber respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information you provide (such as email if you sign up).
  • Subscription and purchase information (processed securely by Apple).
  • App usage data (e.g., sounds played, settings).
  • Device information (e.g., iOS version, crash reports).

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve app functionality.
  • To process subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • To respond to support requests.

3. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Apple manages subscription billing.
  • We may use analytics to improve the app.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

You can manage or cancel subscriptions in your Apple ID settings. To request deletion of support communications, contact us at: slumberhelpdesk@gmail.com

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: slumberhelpdesk@gmail.com
