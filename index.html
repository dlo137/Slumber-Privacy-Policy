Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I purchased a subscription but it isn't active in the app. A: Please try restarting the app. If the issue persists, tap "Restore Purchases" in the app settings. If you still need help, contact us at the support email above.
Q: Why can't I hear any sounds? A: Make sure your device volume is up and the mute switch is off. Also, check if Bluetooth headphones or speakers are connected.
Q: How do I cancel my subscription? A: Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Open the Settings app, tap your name, then "Subscriptions" to manage or cancel.
